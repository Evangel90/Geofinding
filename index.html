About Us

Geofinding LTD, founded by Mr Onosetale Benard Orukpe, a graduate of Geology(B.Tech) from the Federal University Of Technology Minna Niger State, Geological Engineering(HND) from Federal polytechnic Ado, Ekiti State, and Mineral Resources Engineering Technology(ND) from Auchi polytechnic Auchi, Edo State. Geofinding is a Limited Liability Company, registerd with the Coporate Affairs Commision(CAC) domisile in Nigeria with offices in two cities including Abuja and Minna. Geofinding was established to meet the demand for geological and engineering services in Nigeria such as GeoPhysical Survey, Hydrogeological survey(Borehole Survey), Borehole drilling and installation, Geotechnical and engineering investigation, and Mineral exploration e.t.c. We have also extended our services to painting as well as paint production so that people who are looking for quatlity services in any of these aspect will have a one stop shop that fulfills their needs.
